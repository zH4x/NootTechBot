Voting Commands (Admin):
-----------------------
 [{}startvote][TOPIC] - Clears the #Vote chat and begins a new PRIVATE vote with set topic.
 [{}startvote -public][TOPIC] - Clears the #Vote chat and begins a new PUBLIC vote with set topic.
 [{}endvote][] - Ends the vote manually.

Voting Commands:
----------------
 [{}vote][yes] - Submits a 'Yes' vote.
 [{}vote][no] - Submits a 'No' vote.
 [{}vote][topic] - Displays the current vote topic.
 [{}results][] - Displays the results of the vote that has just ended.
